Opeyemi Sanni

The creative swiss army knife.

A Visual artist, Graphic Designer, Composer/Sound Engineer and Technology enthusiast. Mr Sanni is a melting pot of creative expression. His current work can be most briefly described as a post modern take on neo-african expressionism.
Upon deeper observation one can perceive an expression of the old, ancient and historical, through the new, modern, and futuristic (and vise versa).
